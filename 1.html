Floating Island




2021.07 - 2021.08 Floating Island for LH corporation competiton. We tried to create a community island that would be a haeven for drifting young entrepreneurs and beginners in society.

The site is located in Ilsanseo-gu, Goyang-si, next to Ilsan station, Gyeongui Jungang line. Because of that, it is advantageous for connection to forest walkway and also to Ilsan fifth-day market, a historic traditional market. Furthermore, the site is planned to be regenerated for youth start-ups and culture.

The lower floor is actively connecting to the forest walkway of Gyeongui line and it induces access from various visitors. Also, commerciality was enhanced thourgh the extension of Ilsan fifth-day market axis. Therefore, it was intended to increase the utilization of space for youth art creation, exhibition, and sale, which are the main programs of the low-rise area.
