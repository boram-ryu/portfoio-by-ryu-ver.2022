Netflix HQ




2021.03 - 2021.05 Netflix HQ office for academic project. N X N is located in Seogyo-dong, Mapo-gu. It is office building of Netflix Korea and the name of it means Netflix X Networking.

In N X N, Netflix promote content, hold events, and sell products to young people who are the main customers of them and also main visitors of Hongdae. Through these, they create a new network with consumers.

The various interactions that are occurred through the network of Netflix, creators, and consumers work as an element to build creative workplaces.
