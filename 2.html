the Library at Night




2021.01 - 2021.02 the Library at Night for JUNGLIM corporation competiton. Present library is desolate space which only offers books and desks. We tried to create connection between the memory of the book and the people.

The site is located in Jongno-gu. It is also located next to the Tapgol park and Sewoon plaza. We hoped our library hands over quiet hospitality to everyone. It provides comfortable indoor and outdoor community space and various music-focused programs, links with Sewoon plaza, a large musical instrument shopping center.

The bookshelves are terraced type and overall it have dome-shaped. There is a hearing space under the bookshelves. Musicians' busking performances are held in an rooftop outdoor space.
