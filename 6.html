Objet




2019.03 - 2019.04 Objet for academic project. One of the freshman studio activities was to analysis human proportion factors and make objet using the analysed elements.

I analysed one of the gymnastics movement and made diagram focused on major joints such as knees and pelvis. The result of that was shown through fan shapes and triangles.

Afterwards, I made the objet using those figures. The pelvis was taken as the center point and the figures were arranged according to the order of motion.
